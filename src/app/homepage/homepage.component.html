Welcome to the
Spring of Arda





This website was created in order to help familiarise readers of Tolkien's works with the everchanging nature of the maps that were created.


When reading Tolkien's works, especailly The Silmarillion, a reader might be overwhelmed by the amount of information and names presented there. In regards to the political landscapes of Beleriand and Middle-Earth, many changes happen throughout the story. Furthermore, geological transformations sometimes are hard to consider and can't often be depicted with the in book maps.


Here in Spring of Arda, users can freely explore the constant evolution of the world of Tolkien, from it's creation in Ainoulidante to the start of the Fourth Age.
